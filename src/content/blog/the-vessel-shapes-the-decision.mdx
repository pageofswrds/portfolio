#### What does it mean to make a decision?

Why do we do the things we do? and why do we feel compelled to say the things we say?

If you think about it, you can always kind of find a reason. There's always some ancestor — some *thing* that influences our behaviour:

- The neglected kid bullies his peers at school.
- An anxious AuDHDer fumbles a social situation.
- A depressed internet dweller leans into an experience that makes him feel alive.

We like to think that we choose the life we live, but is that really true? So much of it is out of our control. Sudden bills fall into our lap. Panic wreaks havoc through our nerves. The calcification of trauma cements our hearts, dissociating us before we begin to feel our feelings.

The waves of life are vigorous with energy, shifting with amplitude, nudging our trajectories into places we could not have foreseen. And those waves are at the origin of every decision we make.

### The goal is to get good at surfing

While we all know what it feels like to be at the whims of our hormones, we still have the potential to direct their path. Within each of us is an inner flame; a divine spark; a deep sense of *knowing* what feels right. And if we're lucky, that knowledge overrides the surface level energies at place.

> The goal is to follow your North Star

Perhaps you're bipolar and track your cycles of energy. You alternate between high- and low-energy states every other week, in a pattern that follows the 28-day cycle of the moon.

When you know your body is in contraction, you realize it does no good to force specific labor. The mind has a mind of its own, and sometimes it needs to let its attentions run undirected. What looks to the outside a meaningless spinning of wheels is, in actuality, a private devotion to self-maintenance. And you can do so freely, because when you trust that when the time is right, you'll be able to take back the wheel with renewed accuracy & precision.

## The shape of this wave becomes the vessel for the next

There is a deeper conversation about fate v. free will - stay tuned for that, if u wish!

Surfing is all about how you lean; its capacity for expression (technique) is loose, and wide, and you are best off if you don't think about technique and just let the wave take you where it takes you.

You don't get to choose where you go. All you have is trust—that if you send your weight a certain way, your momentum will carry, and hold.

*  ▎ The waves are not yours to choose. But every wave you've ever surfed is still in your legs. The way you leaned, what you carried, where the energy moved through you — all of it folded into the vessel that meets the next wave. The vessel shapes the decision. Not by choosing, but by being the thing that was shaped by everything before. *
